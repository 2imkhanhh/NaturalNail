POLICY

BOOKING POLICY

Please call us directly to schedule your appointment with our staff.

If a card on file is declined or there are any unpaid fees, clients may be kindly asked to update their payment method, settle the balance, and pay a deposit before booking future appointments.

Details regarding appointment confirmation will be sent via email or SMS.

Please arrive 10 minutes prior to your scheduled appointment time.

All fees are listed in our service menu and are subject to change.

CANCELLATION & REFUSAL POLICY

cancel or reschedule at least 24 hours in advance to avoid charges.

Cancellations made within 24 hours of your appointment will incur a 50% service fee.

A no-show occurs when a client is more than 10 minutes late without notice or does not show up at all. This will result in a 100% service fee.

WARRANTY INFORMATION

At Natural Nails & Beauty, we are committed to delivering a refined and high-quality experience. Please review our warranty policy below

Due to the nature of regular nail polish, we do not provide a warranty for this service.

All other services include a 72-hour warranty. If you experience issues such as chipping, discoloration, or technical imperfections within this period, please contact us as soon as possible so we can assist you.

Please notify us within 72 hours after your appointment if any issue arises. After reporting, kindly visit the studio within 48 hours so we can assess and resolve the matter.

Our warranty does not cover:
• Normal wear and tear.
• Damage caused by external factors (impact, chemicals, improper care, etc.).
